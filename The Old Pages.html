About Me

I'm interested in everything, with very few exceptions. I write and read science fiction, fantasy, and anything speculative. I speak enough languages for half my fingers, have enough books to found a small library, and love to talk about ideas. I love the natural world, and go out in it as often as possible.

This portfolio will contain many of the works I produce during my time at the GW Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. You can find my projects in the Portfolio tab above; feel free also to take a look at my Github for more. I'm also available professionally on LinkedIn and AngelList. If you feel like you would like to reach out to me professionally, please do not hesitate to do so.

In the meantime, as I do not yet have any projects to share, please feel free to look at my writing. I've got a few short stories and a poem published. Feel free to head on over to my Writing Page to learn more, or to visit my Author Pages on Amazon and Goodreads.
